# skills-guidelines

Behavioral coding-guideline skill packs.

The Karpathy guidelines are the load-bearing one and are also exported as an
always-on rule at `packages/harness-core/rules/karpathy.md` so they apply on
every conversation, not just when invoked via the skill.

When you change the four principles in `skills/karpathy-guidelines/SKILL.md`,
keep `packages/harness-core/rules/karpathy.md` in sync.
